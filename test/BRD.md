# BRD

## Business Objective
Enable reliable and timely analytics from mixed-format enterprise data.

## KPI Targets
- 95% data freshness SLA
- <1% daily pipeline failures
- Reduced analyst wait time for insights

## Stakeholders
Business analysts, data engineers, platform team, security/compliance.

## Requirements
- Unified ingestion for files and events
- Trusted curated datasets
- Self-service analytics
- Secure and auditable platform
