# BRD (V2)

## Business Objective
Enable reliable, near-real-time analytics from mixed-format enterprise data.

## KPI Targets
- Freshness SLA met for 95% datasets
- <1% daily failures
- Faster analyst self-service access

## Stakeholders
Business analysts, data engineers, platform, security/compliance.

## Business Requirements
- Unified ingestion for files + events
- Trusted curated datasets
- Self-service analytics and dashboards
- Secure and auditable platform
